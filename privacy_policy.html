Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

At Elegets Electronics ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI Workspace and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with a personalized and efficient experience. This includes:

1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you register for an account, we collect the following personal details:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Age
  • City/Location
  • Profile Picture

1.2 Usage and Technical Data

  • Chat History: We store your conversation history locally on your device and transmit it to our AI backend to generate responses.
  • Files and Attachments: If you upload documents or images for analysis, these files are processed to extract text. Profile pictures are stored securely using cloud storage services.
  • Authentication Data: We use authentication providers (Google/Firebase) to verify your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for specific, legitimate purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: To create your account, manage your login session, and generate AI responses based on your inputs.
  • To Personalize Experience: To address you by name and tailor technical explanations to your context.
  • Communication: To send welcome emails, password reset links, or important service updates.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the accuracy and speed of our AI models.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS).
  • Secure Storage: User account data is stored in secure Firebase databases. Profile images are stored in Cloudinary.
  • Local Storage: Your chat history is stored locally in your browser's "Local Storage" for quick access and privacy. Clearing your browser cache will remove this history from your device.

4. Sharing of Information

Elegets Electronics does not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers essential to operating the app:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and database hosting.
  • Cloudinary: For hosting profile images.
  • AI Providers: Text inputs are sent to our Large Language Model (LLM) providers to generate answers. These providers do not use your data for training their public models without consent.

5. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your profile data within the application settings.
  • Correction: You may update your profile information at any time.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting our support team.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for engineering students and hobbyists. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
